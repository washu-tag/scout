The import Job runs once per Helm install/upgrade. Watch progress with:

    kubectl logs -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} \
      -l app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }},component=dashboard-import \
      --tail=200 -f

Whenever any file under analytics/superset/ changes, the rendered ConfigMap's
sha256sum changes, the import Job pod template's checksum/config annotation
changes, and Helm replaces the Job on the next `helm upgrade`. No Ansible
glue required.
